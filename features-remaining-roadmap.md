## Features not in the Roadmap, to consider

- **SEO (Search Engine Optimization)**
  Improve the platform's visibility and discoverability on search engines.
- **Account Registration integration (for federated & builtin users)**
  Extend the authentication system to support both federated identity providers (e.g., Google, GitHub, OIDC) and internal accounts with authentication.
- **Load testing**
  Perform comprehensive load and stress testing to evaluate system scalability, stability, and performance under realistic and peak traffic conditions.
- **Automation of Keycloak configuration**
  Automate the provisioning and management of Keycloak realms, clients, roles, groups, identity providers, and authentication flows so new installations can configure Keycloak reliably with minimal manual setup.
